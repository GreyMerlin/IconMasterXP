Zooming or Magnification

You can change the magnification level of the preview pane (upper viewport) by zooming in or out. To zoom an image:

  • Through main menu, click Edit → Zoom → [Zoom Level]
    Where [Zoom Level] is displayed in the application status bar.
    - Or -
    Press F2, F3, F4 or F5 keyboard shortcuts to cycle between different zoom levels.

You can turn on anti-aliasing feature so preview will be rendered with greater level of quality and detail, to do this:

  • Through main menu, click Edit → Zoom → Enable Anti-Alias

Notes

  1. Image will always fit the preview window no matter what zoom level is used, so you would need to maximize the window to reach actual zoom level with larger images. This cannot be changed since IconMasterXP is designed for icons that are usually not larger than 12x128 pixels.
